PhD Gul Tahaoglu

Department of Computer Engineering, Karadeniz Technical University (KTU)

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Karadeniz Technical University (KTU). My research interests primarily lie at the intersection of Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and Multimedia Forensics.

I specifically focus on developing advanced analysis methods for Deepfake detection and ensuring the reliability of digital image and audio signals using deep learning techniques.
