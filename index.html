About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Engineering, Karadeniz Technical University (KTU). My research bridges Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and Multimedia Forensics.

I am particularly passionate about Deepfake Detection and Digital Media Integrity. My work focuses on developing AI techniques for tasks where data integrity is compromised, such as identifying manipulated audio/video content and analyzing signal anomalies in multimedia files.

Research Highlights

AI-Based Deepfake Detection

Developing robust architectures (like ResNeXt) to detect synthetic audio/video generated by modern generative AI models.

Multimedia Forensics

Analysis of digital footprints in media files to detect forgery, copy-move attacks, and spectral anomalies.
